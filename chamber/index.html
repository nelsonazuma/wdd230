Company Spotlights

  • MTN Ghana: MTN Ghana is the leading telecommunications company in Ghana, with over 25 million subscribers. They offer a wide range of services, including mobile voice and data, mobile money, and fixed broadband. Their advertisement could highlight their latest products and services, or their commitment to providing quality and affordable telecommunications services to Ghanaians.
  • GCB Bank: GCB Bank is the largest bank in Ghana, with over 160 branches and ATMs across the country. They offer a wide range of banking products and services, including personal and business accounts, loans, and investments. Their advertisement could highlight their financial strength and stability, or their wide range of products and services.
  • AngloGold Ashanti: AngloGold Ashanti is a global gold mining company with operations in Ghana, South Africa, Mali, Guinea, and Tanzania. They are the largest gold producer in Ghana and contribute significantly to the country's economy. Their advertisement could highlight their commitment to sustainable mining and their economic impact on Ghana.
