Chamber of Commerce Website Plan

Site Name

The site will be named "Your Town Chamber of Commerce" to represent our local town and its business community. We chose this name to emphasize our town's identity and purpose.

Site Purpose

The website will serve two primary purposes:

  1. To promote local businesses and economic development in Your Town.
  2. To provide valuable resources, networking opportunities, and information to our members and the community.

Scenarios

  1. Business Networking Events: "What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?"
  2. Board of Directors: "Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?"
  3. Population Growth: "What has been the population growth in the area?"

Color Schema

Our color scheme will reflect professionalism and a sense of community:

  • Primary Color: Used for headings and accents.
  • Secondary Color: Used for backgrounds and highlights.
