Accra Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The site will be named "Accra Chamber of Commerce" to represent the capital city of Ghana and its business community.

Site Purpose

The website will serve two primary purposes:

  1. Facilitate networking and collaboration among local businesses in Accra and promote economic growth in the region.
  2. Provide residents and investors with information about Accra's business environment, events, and economic development initiatives.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the recent business growth trends in Accra?

Color Schema

The color scheme for the website will reflect Accra's culture and vibrancy:

  • #0078D4: Used for headings and primary call-to-action buttons.
  • #F0F0F0: Used for background elements, accents, and navigation.

Typography

The typography for the website will maintain a clean and modern look:

  • Primary Font: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif: Used for headings and body text.
